**Research request:** Senya → Victor pricing research

---

## Job Definition

Typical work scope (as described):

1. Remove storefront glass door (aluminum rail + tempered glass, often 200–450 lb)
2. Remove failed floor closer from in-floor cement case / pocket
3. Install new floor closer (align spindle, spring power, valves)
4. Rehang door, adjust closing/latch/backcheck, test cycles
5. Clean up, verify ADA opening force if applicable

**Important distinction:** This is **not** a surface-mounted overhead closer (LCN 1460/4040 on door header). Herculite / CRL / narrow-stile aluminum storefront doors almost always use a **floor-embedded hydraulic closer** hidden below the pivot point.

### LCN (relevant but different product class)

LCN is dominant for **surface-mounted** and **overhead concealed** closers on aluminum storefront headers (1250/1450/1460 series, ~$450–$600 list).

For **true floor closers**, LCN is less common than CRL Jackson / Rixson on glass pivot storefronts in Southern California. If the door uses overhead concealed LCN in the top rail, pricing and labor differ (less demolition, ~1–2 hr job).

**LCN 1461 surface closer list:** ~$582 (wholesale ~$400) — useful reference only if the building uses header-mounted closers, not floor pockets.

**Source:** [LCN Price Book (Allegion)](https://us.allegion.com/content/dam/allegion-us-2/web-files/lcn/price-book/LCN_Price_Book_113462.pdf)

## 2. Labor Time — Experienced Glazier Crew (2 technicians)

Floor closer replacement is a **specialty job**, not a 45-minute surface-closer swap.

| Phase | Time (2-man crew) | Notes |
|-------|-------------------|-------|
| Site prep, tools, door protection | 15 – 20 min | Often during business hours — care for public traffic |
| Remove door (glass + rail) | 45 – 60 min | 2 techs + suction cups / bar hoist; Herculite may need Quattro lift |
| Access & remove old closer | 30 – 60 min | If cement case intact, faster; corrosion adds time |
| Install new closer in pocket | 45 – 90 min | Spindle height, centering ±3°, spring size 1–6 |
| Rehang door, align pivots | 45 – 75 min | Critical — binding risks glass contact |
| Adjust valves, test 20+ cycles | 20 – 40 min | Closing speed, latch, backcheck, hold-open if applicable |
| **Total on-site** | **3.0 – 5.5 hours** | **6 – 11 man-hours** |

**Industry context:**

- Surface-mounted closer replacement: ~1–2 hours (national guides, locksmith averages)
- Floor-mounted closer: significantly more — floor pocket access, door removal, pivot alignment ([National Lock Supply installation guide](https://nationallocksupply.com/blog/how-to-install-a-door-closer-complete-professional-installation-guide/))
- Herculite / all-glass: often **two visits** if custom parts must be ordered (temp repair + permanent fix)

### LA labor billing (commercial glazing)

| Rate basis | Range | This job (4 hr × 2 tech) |
|------------|-------|--------------------------|
| Shop billable rate / tech | $95 – $150/hr | $760 – $1,200 |
| Flat labor (Alex benchmark) | — | **~$900** |
| Premium (emergency, weekend, Beverly Hills / high-rise logistics) | +25 – 40% | $1,100 – $1,600 |

**Alex's ~$900 labor figure is realistic** for a straightforward CRL Jackson replacement in LA when:

- Existing floor pocket is usable (no concrete demolition)
- No custom arm fabrication required
- Door removes/rehangs without complications
- Normal business-hours service

Add **$200 – $500 labor** if: corroded case, pocket rebuild, intermediate pivot replacement, or after-hours emergency.

## 3. Los Angeles Market — What Others Charge

National averages for "commercial closer replacement $300–$800" refer to **surface-mounted** units, **not** floor closers ([Commercial Door Closer Repair Pros](https://commercialdoorcloserrepair.com/commercial-door-closer-repair-costs)).

LA-specific factors:

- Labor 30–60% above national rural average ([Door Repair Authority 2025](https://doorrepairauthority.com/door-repair-cost-guide/))
- Commercial glass door specialty shops quote **project-based**, not hourly
- Emergency glass/door services (S.O.S., JDM, Westpoint) emphasize site assessment + free estimate model

Comparable LA-adjacent services (reference only):

| Service type | Typical range |
|--------------|---------------|
| Commercial door repair (general) | $150 – $600 |
| Door frame repair | $250 – $900 |
| Door hardware replacement | $100 – $300 |
| Automatic operator repair | $200 – $700 |

Floor closer on storefront sits **above** these — it's closer to **light commercial door system work** ($800–$2,500+) due to door removal and embedded hardware.

## 7. Confidence & Caveats

| Factor | Confidence | Impact on price |
|--------|------------|-----------------|
| CRL Jackson 900 pricing | **High** — multiple public price lists | Low variance |
| Rixson L27 pricing | **High** — distributor listings | +$400–$800 vs CRL |
| Labor hours | **Medium** — site-dependent | ±$200–$400 |
| Herculite custom hardware | **Low without site visit** | +$300–$1,000 |
| LA market premium | **Medium** — limited public storefront-specific comps | Supports $2K+ total |

**Always confirm on site before final quote:**

- Brand/model stamped on closer body (if accessible)
- Center-hung vs 3/4" offset
- Hold-open vs no hold-open
- Door weight / width (Jackson 900 = up to 1,000 lb / 54" center-hung)
- Condition of cement case and pivot hardware
